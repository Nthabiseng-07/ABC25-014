Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and express shipping (2-3 business days). Delivery times start from the order processing date, which is typically 1 business day. Weekend and holiday delays may apply.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 10-15 business days. Customs fees and import duties may apply depending on your country.

Can I track my order?

Yes! You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can use this number to track your package in real-time on the carrier's website.

Sizing & Fit

How do I know what size to order?

Check our detailed size chart on each product page. Our streetwear collection runs true to size, though some vintage-inspired pieces have a relaxed fit. Customer reviews also include sizing feedback.

Can I exchange an item for a different size?

Absolutely! If you receive the wrong size, contact our customer service within 30 days. We'll arrange a free size exchange. Just keep the item unworn with original tags.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags attached, and in original packaging. Sale items and clearance products are final sale.

How do I start a return?

Log into your account and go to "My Orders". Select the item you want to return and click "Request Return". Print the prepaid shipping label and drop off your package at any shipping location. Your refund will be processed 5-7 days after we receive it.

Will I be charged for return shipping?

No! We provide a free prepaid shipping label for all returns (except final sale items). Simply use the label we provide to return your item.

Product Care

How do I care for my graphic tees?

Wash in cold water (inside out for graphic prints), use mild detergent, and avoid bleach. Tumble dry on low heat or air dry for best results. This helps preserve the color and graphic quality of your apparel.

Will the graphics fade or crack?

Our high-quality graphics are designed to last for years with proper care. Follow our washing instructions to minimize fading. Any defects due to manufacturing will be covered under our quality guarantee.

Orders & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Can I cancel my order?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, you can cancel it within 24 hours. Contact our customer service immediately with your order number. Orders already in transit cannot be cancelled.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! Digital and physical gift cards are available in various denominations. They never expire and can be used on any product in our shop.
